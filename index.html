Our Mission

Who We Are

Founded in 2026, TrashAway began with a simple observation: Nigeria needed a better way to manage waste. Traditional methods were inefficient, unreliable, and harmful to our environment.

We built TrashAway to change that. Our platform connects households and businesses with verified waste collectors, making pickup convenient, transparent, and eco-friendly — from Ibadan to the entire nation.

Sustainability First

Every decision prioritizes environmental impact. We aim for 90% waste recycling by 2027.

Community Focused

We partner with local collectors, create real jobs, and invest in communities Nigeria-wide.

Transparency & Trust

Real-time tracking, verified agents, and detailed reporting — you know exactly what happens to your waste.

Our Story

Our Journey

From a small startup in Ibadan to Nigeria's most trusted waste management platform — in record time.

2026

Founded TrashAway

Our founding team recognized Nigeria's growing waste crisis and decided to build a technology-driven solution. We started in Ibadan with a team of 10 verified collectors and a mission to make sustainable waste management accessible to everyone.

2026

Expanded to 5 Major Cities

High demand led us to rapidly launch in Lagos, Kano, Port Harcourt, and Abuja. Within months we completed over 25,000 pickups and recycled more than 10,000 tons of waste, proving the model worked at scale.

2026

50,000+ Pickups Milestone

TrashAway grew into Nigeria's most trusted waste management platform with 10,000+ active users, 500+ verified collectors across the nation, and an 80% waste recycling rate — we're just getting started.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about TrashAway.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes! There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your Pro subscription anytime from your account settings. Your cancellation takes effect immediately with no penalties or hidden fees.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major debit and credit cards, bank transfers, and mobile money options including MTN, Airtel, and 9Mobile. All transactions are secure and end-to-end encrypted.
How do Eco Points work?
Every pickup earns Eco Points based on the weight and type of waste sorted and recycled. Pro plan users earn 2× points per pickup. You can redeem points for discounts on future pickups, cash back, or donate them to environmental restoration projects across Nigeria.
Which cities are currently covered?
We currently operate in Lagos, Abuja, Kano, Port Harcourt, Ibadan, and over 30 other locations across all 36 states and FCT. If you're unsure, enter your address on sign-up and we'll confirm availability instantly.
Can I join as a waste collector?
Absolutely! If you own a truck or operate a waste management company anywhere in Nigeria, you can partner with TrashAway to get consistent jobs, real-time routing, and guaranteed monthly earnings. Sign up as a Collector on our platform.
Do you offer annual billing discounts?
Yes! Pay annually and save 15% on the Pro plan — that's ₦25,000 instead of ₦30,000. You can switch to annual billing from your account settings at any time.
